About Cedars-Gerizim Medical Center

Delivering compassionate, professional healthcare to your home since our founding. We believe everyone deserves access to quality medical care in the comfort of their own space.

Our Mission

To provide safe, accessible, affordable, and compassionate healthcare services 24/7, bringing professional medical care directly to patients' homes. We are committed to improving the quality of life for our patients through personalized, comprehensive healthcare solutions.

Our team of dedicated healthcare professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every patient receives the highest standard of care, whether through home visits, telemedicine consultations, or our comprehensive subscription packages.

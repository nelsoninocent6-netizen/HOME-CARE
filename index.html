About Us

Who We Are

Cedars Gerizim Medical Center is a leading healthcare institution dedicated to providing comprehensive, compassionate, and cutting-edge medical care. Founded over 15 years ago, we have grown into a trusted name serving thousands of patients across the region with a commitment to excellence, innovation, and patient-first values.

🎯

Our Mission

To deliver safe, high-quality, affordable healthcare to every individual, regardless of location or circumstance.

👁️

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative healthcare provider, setting the standard for home-based and community care.

💎

Our Values

Integrity, compassion, excellence, teamwork, and continuous improvement in all that we do.
